FRUIT ZU 🍹
Refreshing Brand Identity & Product Design Project

Fruit Zu is a vibrant fruit drink brand concept designed to feel fresh, youthful, energetic, and visually memorable.
The project focuses on building a playful yet marketable beverage identity through logo design, can packaging, posters, and promotional visuals.
